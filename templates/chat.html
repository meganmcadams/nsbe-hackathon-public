Welcome to Charripm!

The idea of this web app is to allow users to chat with any character of their choice using ChatGPT for text and FakeYou for voice synthesization.

Using this web application, you can speak to one of five fictional characters: Yoda, Spongebob, Harry Potter, Walter White, and Shrek.

To pick a different character to talk to, select a character from the drop-down at the top-left of the screen.

To chat, type a message in the textbox at the bottom of the screen and click "Enter" on your keyboard or "Send" on the screen.

For feedback with grammar or spelling, check the checkbox beneath the textbox.
